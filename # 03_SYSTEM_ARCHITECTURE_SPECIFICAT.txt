# CHAPTER 1

# ARCHITECTURAL VISION

---

## Purpose

This document defines the technical blueprint of the Fashion & Apparel eCommerce Platform.

It establishes the complete architectural standards governing software structure, service interaction, infrastructure, scalability, maintainability, performance, security, deployment, monitoring, and future extensibility.

Every engineering decision shall follow this specification.

---

## Vision

Build a globally scalable Fashion & Apparel eCommerce platform capable of supporting millions of customers while maintaining:

• Excellent User Experience

• Excellent Performance

• High Availability

• Enterprise Security

• Easy Maintainability

• Modular Growth

• AI Readiness

without requiring fundamental architectural redesign.

# ARCHITECTURAL CONSTRAINTS

The following are mandatory:

The frontend shall never communicate directly with PostgreSQL.

The frontend shall never contain business rules.

The backend shall never expose internal implementation details.

The database shall never be accessed outside repositories.

All external integrations must pass through service abstractions.

Every architectural decision must prioritize long-term maintainability over short-term convenience.

---

# GLOBAL AI ARCHITECTURE RESTRICTIONS

The AI coding agent shall never:

Replace the approved architecture.

Convert the project into microservices.

Introduce undocumented dependencies.

Change service boundaries.

Mix architectural patterns.

Implement AI features.

Create undocumented integrations.

If architectural clarification is required,

STOP.

Request guidance from the project owner.
